Our Values
Radical Trust
Camp Inno focuses on trust as a foundation to all relationships within our community. Trust becomes radical when we allow candor to thrive within our community, where folks can authentically communicate and express themselves, through conflict or otherwise, to move forward stronger, and together.
Tailored Care
We strive to ensure each individual member of our community feels cared for. And we acknowledge that everyone has varying ways they need to be taken care of. Tailored care allows us to go beyond a one-size-fits-all approach to ensure we meet the individual needs of the folks in our community.
Restorative Innovation
RI is a level up to the Entrepreneurial Mindset, fostering a desire to innovate in ways that address long standing challenges, creating a world that provides adequate resources and care for more (all) folks.
Tangible Access
Camp Inno is dedicated to making camp as easy to prepare for, get to, and feel safe at as we possibly can. We want to remove the barriers to overnight camp so that more kid and staff can enjoy the magic of camp.

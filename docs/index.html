Camp Inno

A new kind of camp is taking shape.

Camp Inno is building a premium summer experience where creativity, innovation, and belonging actually live in the same place.

So, You Think You Can Camp? Meet the Team
Mission

Removing barriers for the next generation of innovators.

Camp Inno exists to make high-trust, high-creativity camp experiences feel possible, welcoming, and worth building.

Radical Trust

Build a community where candor, care, and accountability can coexist.

Tailored Care

Support people as individuals instead of forcing everyone into one camp mold.

Restorative Innovation

Create ideas that repair, expand access, and move beyond surface novelty.

Tangible Access

Reduce the real barriers that keep people from getting to camp and belonging there.

Programs

Three tracks. One ambitious camp experience.

Technology

Code, robotics, and digital problem-solving for campers who want to build.

Multimedia

Film, photography, design, and storytelling for campers who want to create.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Big ideas, financial literacy, and venture thinking for campers who want to lead.

Atmosphere

Creative energy, time outdoors, and the kind of momentum that lingers after camp ends.

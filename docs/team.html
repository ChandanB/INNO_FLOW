Meet The Team

Mission


‍In order to ensure a successful transition into the future of employment, our mission is to prepare the next generation of individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge. We believe that the job market is continuously evolving, and it is our responsibility to equip young minds with the tools they need to thrive and succeed. By offering innovative educational programs and opportunities, we aim to empower students to become adaptable, critical thinkers who are capable of embracing change and navigating the challenges of tomorrow. Our goal is to foster a learning environment that cultivates creativity, fosters collaboration, and encourages lifelong learning. Through our dedication and commitment, we strive to shape the leaders of tomorrow and contribute to a brighter and more prosperous future for all.

Vision

‍We are committed to creating a better world with improved business opportunities by providing the necessary skills to the next generation. Our mission is to equip young individuals with the knowledge and expertise needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving global market. Through innovative educational programs, we empower students with essential entrepreneurial and leadership skills, enabling them to contribute to sustainable development and economic growth. By fostering a culture of creativity, innovation, and ethical business practices, we aim to shape the future leaders who will drive positive change in society. Our comprehensive curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration, equipping students with the tools to address complex challenges and seize emerging opportunities. Together, we can build a brighter future and create a more prosperous world for generations to come.

Meet The Team

Nelson Strickland
CO-FOUNDER

Nelson is a multi-talented youth development professional from New Jersey who began attending summer camps at the age of 11. He fell in love with the community and empowering environment summer camp creates and hasn’t spent a summer without it since.

Nelson has aided in the advancement of over 20 camps and is called upon for his creative programming skills. Beyond his professional pursuits, Nelson enjoys music production, gaming, cooking, and basketball.

Paige Moffet
CO-FOUNDER

With 10+ years of experience in Youth Development, Paige currently runs Operations for Change Summer, where she has helped open 4 overnight summer camps, and supports Operations and Compliance for 5 camps serving over 2,500 campers.

Paige also helps The Summer Camp Society part time with operations.

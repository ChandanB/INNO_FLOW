Inno Technology

Computer Programming & Robotics

The Technology track at Camp Inno immerses campers in the expansive field of digital creation and problem-solving. It covers everything from coding in popular programming languages and developing custom software, to constructing and programming robots, and mastering the art of UI/UX design to enhance user interactions with technology.

This track may be for you if your camper...

‍- Loves tinkering with technology and have a knack for problem-solving.
- Is fascinated by the inner workings of computers and robots.
- Enjoys bringing ideas to life through coding and building.
- Thrives in collaborative environments and enjoy working on projects with others.

Computer Programming

keyboard_arrow_down
In our Computer Programming program, campers dive deep into the world of coding. They'll learn languages like Python, Java, and Scratch, gaining hands-on experience with fundamental programming concepts such as variables, loops, functions, and conditionals. Through interactive projects and challenges, they'll unleash their creativity while honing their problem-solving skills.

Robotics

keyboard_arrow_down
In Robotics, campers get hands-on experience building and programming robots using LEGO Mindstorms or similar platforms. They'll explore the principles of robotics, learning about sensors, motors, and actuators as they design and build their own robots. Through exciting challenges and competitions, they'll put their creations to the test and see firsthand how code brings robots to life.

Inno Multimedia

Film & Photography

The Multimedia track at Camp Inno offers campers a comprehensive journey through various forms of digital artistic expression. Participants learn the intricacies of filmmaking, from conceptualization to editing, develop critical photography skills including composition and editing, and dive into digital animation to bring imaginative stories and characters to animated life.

Your camper may thrive in our Multimedia track if they:

- Have a passion for visual storytelling, whether through movies or photography.
- Enjoy taking on various roles and planning projects, embracing the creative process.
- Demonstrate a knack for creativity and a unique perspective on the world.
- Love working with technical equipment and have a keen eye for detail in capturing moments.

Filmography

keyboard_arrow_down
Our Filmmaking program is where the magic truly happens. Picture this: your camper collaborates from start to finish, crafting their own original short films. From brainstorming wild ideas to bringing them to life on screen, every step is an adventure.We equip our campers with top-notch gear like DSLR cameras and industry-standard editing software—think Adobe Premiere and Davinci Studio. But we don't stop there. With options for boom mics, green screens, and more, we're all about turning imagination into cinematic reality.

Photography

keyboard_arrow_down
We believe that every moment holds the potential for artistic expression. In our Digital Photography program, we empower young campers to capture the world around them through the lens of a camera, fostering a lifelong passion for creativity and storytelling.Campers will learn the fundamentals of photography, how to use a DSLR camera, and how to edit their photos using industry-standard software, Adobe Photoshop or Lightroom. Combining these skills with their specific interests, campers will also learn the basics of successful marketing through layout design using InDesign. Beautifully taken pictures will be used in a wide range of possible camper projects, including posters, infographics/brochures, magazines/newsletters, product labels, print advertisements, comic strips, calendars, branding, and interactive print media.

Inno Innovation

Entrepreneurship & Financial Literacy

The Inno track at Camp Inno is designed to cultivate a range of modern skills relevant to today's digital and entrepreneurial landscapes. Campers participate in competitive esports, enhancing strategic thinking and teamwork, and delve into financial literacy and entrepreneurship, gaining knowledge from basic financial concepts to comprehensive business planning.

Entrepreneurship & Financial Literacy

keyboard_arrow_down
Campers dive deep into the realms of entrepreneurship and financial literacy, exploring what it takes to turn innovative ideas into successful ventures. Through interactive workshops, engaging activities, and real-world simulations, they'll develop essential skills and knowledge that will serve them well in their future endeavors.

A highlight of the week is the "Dolphin Tank", where campers have the opportunity to pitch their business ideas to a panel of mentors and peers. This experience not only encourages creativity and critical thinking but also builds confidence and public speaking skills.

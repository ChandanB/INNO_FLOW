Our Values
Radical Trust
Camp Inno focuses on trust as a foundation to all relationships within our community. Trust becomes radical when we allow candor to thrive within our community, where folks can authentically communicate and express themselves, through conflict or otherwise, to move forward stronger, and together.
Tailored Care
We strive to ensure each individual member of our community feels cared for. And we acknowledge that everyone has varying ways they need to be taken care of. Tailored care allows us to go beyond a one-size-fits-all approach to ensure we meet the individual needs of the folks in our community.
Restorative Innovation
RI is a level up to the Entrepreneurial Mindset, fostering a desire to innovate in ways that address long standing challenges, creating a world that provides adequate resources and care for more (all) folks.
Tangible Access
Camp Inno is dedicated to making camp as easy to prepare for, get to, and feel safe at as we possibly can. We want to remove the barriers to overnight camp so that more kid and staff can enjoy the magic of camp.
Goals
3 Distinct & Tailored Tracks

Camp Inno offers three distinct tracks, each tailored to cater to the diverse interests and passions of our campers:

1. Technology 2. Multimedia 3. Innovation & Entrepreneurship
HOW

Balance

Camp Inno strikes the perfect balance between structured learning and free exploration. Campers participate in focused sessions within their chosen tracks, guided by experienced specialists. Additionally, they enjoy unstructured time to collaborate, create, and engage with each other under the guidance of our supportive staff.

Why

CREATIVITY & BELONGING

Beyond the learning, Camp Inno is a vibrant community where ideas flourish, projects come to life, and lifelong friendships are forged. Picture this – a collective brainstorming session under the canopy of trees, projects taking flight like soaring kites, and bonds forming around the crackling campfire. Our grand all-camp adventures aren't just activities; they're the threads knitting together an environment of imagination and camaraderie, crafting memories that will echo through the rustling leaves well beyond our week in the woods.
